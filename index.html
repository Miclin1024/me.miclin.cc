Highlight Projects

Dark Sky

The last one-week mini-project of the MDB training program. The lightweight App was built to present weather data from the Dark Sky API.

It automatically retrieves user's current physical location and allows the user to search for locations and store them for future access.

In this project, I explored some third party libraries such as lottie (vector animations) and GMS API (location lookup & filtering).

XMDA.CC

XMDA is a debate alliance I started back in my high school days in China, it carries the vision of bringing people, oppotunities, and resources in this community closer together.

Which is why I created xmda.cc, a website used by clubs and individuals to as a teaching terminal and resource sharing hub.

The website was built with wordpress as most staff in the clubs who are running the contents do not have programming experience.

The backend architecture is MYSQL and NGINX with HTTPS support.

www.xmda.cc
